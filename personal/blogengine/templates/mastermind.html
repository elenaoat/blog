Nordic Masterminds is a mastermind initiative aimed to the socio-economical region of Northern Europe. This area mainly includes Iceland, Norway, Denmark, Finland, Russia and Estonia. 

 

The topic and ambition behind Nordic Masterminds is self-development in business and in life. Whether it’s finding better ways of leading your company, finally launching a product or getting your life to the next level.

Mastermind groups are a fairly new concept to many people though the concept has been around for over 75 years. They are a lot more frequently found in the USA and it is our mission to bring them to the Nordics.  The beauty of Mastermind Groups is that participants raise the bar by challenging each other to create and implement goals, brainstorm ideas, and support each other with total honesty, respect and compassion. Participants act as catalysts for growth, devil’s advocates and supportive colleagues.

We are looking for high-performing individuals that want to take responsibility of their success and be accountable in doing so. The group will consist from 2 to 8 members that share similar ambitions and are committed for bi-weekly virtual meetups.


Due to the fact that the group should possess certain amount of homogeneity within it, please see if you match some of the below attributes. These are also some things that reflect, what the group contains at the moment.

  • You live or are striving for the kind of life you want.
  • You’re open and willing to learn new and not afraid of discovering new. 
  • You can take real feedback. 
  • You can be open about your triumphs and failures. 
  • You are hungry for success and never wish to settle. 
  • You are able to sacrifice today for a better tomorrow. 
  • You are committed to what you do. 2-7 other people expect you to deliver for yourself as they do too. 
  • Want to reach and exceed your goals and to be accountable for them. 
  • You want support from your peers to get those extra percents out of you. 
  • You train yourself with books, audio books, podcasts, blogs, articles etc. consistently. Not because you need to, but because you want to.
